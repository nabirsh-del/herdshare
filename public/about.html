Why We Exist

The beef industry is broken. Middlemen extract value. Ranchers struggle. Consumers lose traceability. This is bigger than beef. It's about who controls our food.

p>

HerdShare eliminates the middleman. We connect independent American ranchers directly with buyers who care about quality, values, and transparency. Ranchers focus on raising. Buyers know their source. Everyone wins.
